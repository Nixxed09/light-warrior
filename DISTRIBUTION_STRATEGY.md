# Distribution Strategy

## Position

Light Warrior is a PC/Steam-first third-person arena action roguelite concept.

The first public-facing asset should not be a web link. It should be a short gameplay clip that proves the sacred-circle temple loop.

## First 10 Players

Channel:

- Local playtests.
- Directly observed sessions.
- Target-player friends/family.

Goal:

- Prove first 10 seconds and temple risk.
- Record confusion and voluntary replay.

Traveling asset:

- 10-second capture of circle expansion.
- 30-second capture of Thunder Hammer temple risk.

## First 100 Players

Channel:

- Itch private/unlisted build or Steam playtest later.
- Discord/local community sharing.
- Short clips posted where action roguelite players can react.

Goal:

- Validate replay pull.
- Validate whether the hook is explainable from a clip.

Traveling asset:

- "Leave safety, take the hammer, reclaim the arena" clip.
- Before/after screenshot of circle expansion.

## Store Positioning Draft

Short hook:

```text
Leave the sacred light to claim weapon temples, then return strong enough to push back living darkness.
```

Steam tags to evaluate later:

- Action Roguelite
- Third-Person
- Arena Fighter
- Fantasy
- Controller

## Platform Risk

Primary dependency:

- UE5 production environment.
- Steam/itch distribution after signal.

Risks:

- UE5 scope can balloon before combat signal.
- Marketplace assets can dilute identity.
- Store page is premature without a strong 30-second clip.

Fallback:

- Keep captured clips and standalone builds portable.
- Keep the browser prototype as documentation/evidence only.

## Distribution Gate

Do not create a store page until:

- UE5 vertical slice exists.
- 30-second capture shows the hook.
- At least 5 observed players understand the loop without explanation.
