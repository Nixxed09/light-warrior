# Player Thank You

## What Players Should Thank The Game For

Players should thank Light Warrior for making bravery obvious, fair, and rewarding.

Specific promises:

- "I knew where safety was."
- "I knew why I was taking damage."
- "I felt stronger because I took a risk."
- "The arena changed because of what I did."
- "I wanted to try one more run."

## Trust Rules

Do not break:

- Light means safety and power.
- Darkness means pressure and danger.
- Weapon temples are worth the risk.
- Enemy tells stay readable through VFX.
- Failure feels recoverable, not cheap.

## Most Likely Trust Breakers

- VFX hides enemy tells.
- The circle shrinks without readable cause.
- Leaving the circle drains health too quickly.
- Temple activation feels like a pickup instead of a heroic power spike.
- Camera loses the circle boundary during combat.

## Weekly Player-Facing Improvement Filter

Every next task should improve at least one:

- Input feel.
- Combat feedback.
- Circle readability.
- Temple risk/reward.
- Enemy tell clarity.
- Restart speed.

If a task does not improve one of those, it waits until after first playable signal.

## First Playable Thank-You Target

After the first playable, a player should say:

```text
I got it immediately: I had to leave the light to become strong enough to save it.
```
